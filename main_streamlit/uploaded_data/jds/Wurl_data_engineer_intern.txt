Data Engineer Intern (Remote - US)

Wurl connects the world’s video content to global audiences. Our data platform powers advanced analytics, real-time decision-making, and secure operations across the OTT video streaming ecosystem.

We’re looking for a Data Engineering Intern to assist in enhancing the security and reliability of our platform—specifically focusing on key rotation and credential lifecycle automation. This role will provide hands-on experience in data engineering, security operations, and cloud infrastructure, with guidance from experienced engineers and cross-functional teams.

What You’ll Do

Support the implementation and automation of key rotation policies and secure credential storage across our cloud infrastructure.
Collaborate with the Data Engineering team to ensure proper access control and encryption practices are enforced in all ETL pipelines and data services.
Assist in developing monitoring and alerting systems for key usage and rotation schedules.
Contribute to internal documentation and knowledge bases related to key rotation procedures.
Learn about and contribute to large-scale, cloud-native data systems.

What You Bring

Currently pursuing or recently completed a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Cybersecurity, or related field.
Familiarity with Python and SQL.
Understanding of basic cloud concepts (AWS preferred).
Strong interest in data security, access control, and automation.
Eagerness to learn and contribute to a fast-paced, remote engineering team.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Nice to Have

Coursework or experience with:
Key management services (e.g., AWS KMS, …).
Infrastructure-as-code tools like Terraform.
SOC2, HIPAA, or other security compliance frameworks.
CI/CD pipelines or secure software development practices.
What We Offer

Paid internship ($30/hr) with flexible scheduling.
Remote-first work culture and support.
Hands-on mentorship from senior engineers.
